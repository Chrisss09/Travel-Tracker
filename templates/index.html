Share and track your journey wherever you are!

Have you ever missed your flight? Have you ever got lost on your travels? Have you ever got confused with all of your information not being in one place? All of this makes your holiday plans very stressful. This app will make planning your holiday very easy going and stress free. With this app you will be able to add all your plans in one place. You can add in all your flight and hotel details so you can always refer to this app and never get lost.

Share your experience with other users!

It is not just a holiday tracker, you can share your experiences with other users. By just logging in you can create your review for other users to see.

If you love a particular country, log it here. If you love a particular hotel, log it here. If you find some amazing attractions, just log it here.

Also please add your least pleasant experiences and tell everyone why.

View the map

Visit our map page where you will be able to access this without logging in. Incase of an emergency or you need some help you can just click on it and find what you are looking for.

You can allow the app to track your location, measure distances and view your surroundings.

We have also put some of our top 10 selections on the map for you to visit which includes, best countries, best restraunts, and best attractions, so if you are nearby or planning a great holiday, it will be there for your choice.
